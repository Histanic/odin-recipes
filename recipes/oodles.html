Oodles and Noodles

Description

Yummy noodles made from Ramen and delightful yum yums

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • water
  • flavoring packet
  • egg
  • pork for tonkatsu

Steps

  1. place bowl
  2. empty ramen in bowl
  3. add X amount of water
  4. microwave or boil water then stir in noodles
  5. stir in flavor contents(may be liquid or dry)
  6. Enjoy the wonderful ramen!
